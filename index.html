ABOUT

Cryptocurrency Trading Dashboard

For the past six months I have been working at a startup with some friends and other students at USF called VolaTrade. VolaTrade is a cryptocurrency trading platform that allows for ease of automated back testing, live paper-trading and implementation of live trading strategies. VolaTrade also uses machine learning solutions for providing valuable indicator data. The purpose of this project is to provide a static representation of the data visualizations that will be used for the VolaTrade cryptocurrency trading dashboard. I will provide 3 different visualizations for this project. First, a candlestick chart which shows the chosen crypto asset's open, high, low, and close price for a given time period. Second, a treemap will show the price performance of multiple assets simultaneously. Third a volume bar chart which shows the volume for a given asset for a given time interval.

DASHBOARD

Candlestick Chart

Candlestick chart with interactive zooming, panning, and tooltip
Historical candle data for Bitcoin starting from December 18, 2017
Green and Red are used to indicate a bull and bear bar respectively

1. Double click or mouse wheel to zoom in and out
2. Hold left-click and drag to explore the chart
3. Hover tooltip shows date, open, high, low, and close



Volume Bar Graph

Volume bar graph with interactive zooming, panning, and tooltip
Historical volume data for Ethereum starting from December 19, 2017
Green and Red are used to indicate a bull and bear bar respectively

1. Double click or mouse wheel to zoom in and out
2. Hold left-click and drag to explore the chart
3. Hover tooltip shows date, volume (USDT), and volume (token)



Crypto Token Tree Map

Tree map with diverging color scale, tool tip, and grouping by hashing algorithm
Price performance for 17 different tokens on April 12th, 2021
Green and Red are used to indicate a positive and negative price performance respectively

1. Hover tooltip shows price performance over 1-day, symbol, date
volume (USDT), volume (Token), and the hashing algorithm
